Welcome,

I’m Davide, a data scientist from Italy.

After much thought, I’ve finally created a personal website - a front to my public and professional persona. It’s a space to share my work and the ideas behind it. Some posts will be technical, while others will reflect more personal interests.

In creating this site, I’ve realized that sharing your work can feel like stepping into the light. It’s a chance to grow, not just by refining your skills, but by embracing the vulnerability of being seen.

Here, you’ll find technical notes, reflections on various topics, and updates as this project continues to grow. It’s still a work in progress; in a sense, it always will be.

Thank you for visiting. I hope you enjoy your stay.
